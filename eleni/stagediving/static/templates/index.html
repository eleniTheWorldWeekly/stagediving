ABOUT STAGE DIVING

Like you, we really enjoy live music, especially by unknown, upcoming bands that turn out to be just amazing. Bands that live next door and are passionate about their music, but don't have the resources to market it. For these bands, live concerts are the best way to get in touch with their audience and make a living out of it. For us, live concerts are the best way to start the night and discover something new to listen to.

So if you are a band or an event organizer, just use the Add Event button to upload your gig's poster FOR FREE. We built StageDiving for you, so that we can discover you and enjoy your music.

Feel free to contact us, for any inquiries, or comments and don't forget to like us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter.

In order to enrich our contet we use Last.fm, Youtube, echonest, and csscheckbox.
